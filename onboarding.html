Welcome to PromptGreen

Track your AI savings and impact

What we track

  • Tokens saved when you optimize prompts on AI sites
  • Estimated energy (kWh) and CO₂ (grams) from your usage
  • Session duration and number of actions

Privacy

All data stays on your device. No external servers. We never read your page content by default. You control when to start and end sessions.

How to use

  1. Start Session when you begin using AI tools.
  2. Use ChatGPT, etc. as usual — we count savings from prompt optimization.
  3. End Session when done. Open View Report to see your impact.
Used to estimate carbon intensity of energy. You can change this later in Settings.
